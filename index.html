About me

Hi, I am Elri, some may also know me as Sirius. I am a student at Wits, and this website is for my course, Interactive Media. Currently it is my favourite subject. I did everything with Visual Studio and GitHub. Some of my favourite things are pizza (not original of me), disregarding the canon of the DCU to create my own happy blobs, and creating art for myself. I like to read and write in my spare time, and I have a small black and white dog named James, he's turning 5 in September. I also have a younger brother that I love dearly, but I will not lend him anything I own. My favourite musical artist is Taylor Swift, and my current gaming hyperfixation is Stardew Valley. I really like the simple things in life, no matter how basic.

I am an artist, both with analogical and digital mediums. I am still learning how to code, so I am not 100% confident in any coding language. Many would consider me as a jack of all trades, master of none. But isn't that better than being master of only one? The reason I have so many skills but little mastery is because I am unfortunately a perfectionist, and if I don't know how to do something immediately, I tend to drop the skill. But I am working on getting over the fear of failing!
