IxD process mashup / Metadata

Elri Greyling

My goal for this website is to show people my skills in the world of art in a variety of media. I want to be able to put all my artwork in one place so it is easier for me to view and show someone in case they are interested, and to update the portfolio as I go. Mostly, I want to have a thing to keep my family up to date with my latest artworks without the chore of needing to interact with them. My family is veryâ€¦ interested in my art, to a point where I don't even want to show them art purely because it is such a hassle to keep bringing an entire library to show my grandma. It will also be nice to have a gallery to show my talents in case for job hires and scouts. By showing them what I can do, they'll be more inclined to hire me if they see I can do the job, have the right motivations (love for the craft), and the drive to see things through. The most important on my website would be the portfolio section in the future. Currently, for my course, the blogs are the main priority. The design section will be important for people who are interested in my thought process and how I chose what I did and why I chose it.

Struggles:

Because my mind is very visual, I struggle to not immediately put everything in the right style at this moment. Other than that, I have had no big struggles other than little annoyances.

Proud of:

I love my newer wireframe and I love the vibes I want this website to be. To me, showing my personality through the visuals of the website is important as it is a part of me. And I am kind of proud that I am building this website because it is purely mine, it is not something someone else did for me. I am also proud of my organization in the folders, because everything is sorted and in one place, I get little tingles of joy when I see my organized files instead of one big hybrid of things.

Changes:

From the original wireframes vs the latest wireframes, a lot has changed, the most notable being the navigation bar. I added a hamburger navigation bar to all the sections since I think it looks nicer and easier to navigate. I might make them slightly bigger. Also I decided to make the blocks in the wireframes containing the content to be a little see-through so you can see the cool background too.
